Who We Are

The Shantabai Nimbalkar Remembrance Multipurpose Organisation is a private, self-financed, non-government organisation founded in 1993, named in loving memory of Smt. Shantabai Nimbalkar.

Though private, both schools under our umbrella carry the distinction of being government-recognised institutions — a testament to our quality and integrity.

We accept no external donations, relying entirely on our own resources. Our reach spans Kanhan, Nagpur and Wardha, Parseoni.
